What We Do

Products & Services We Offer

From supply and installation to after-sales maintenance — reliable solutions for security, connectivity, comfort, and safety.

📷 CCTV Systems

CCTV Camera Systems

Supply, installation & maintenance of IP and analog CCTV. HD, PTZ, night-vision, and remote-monitoring setups for homes, offices & commercial sites.

🌐 Networking

Networking & Connectivity

Structured cabling, Wi-Fi installation, router & switch configuration, and full LAN/WAN setup for homes, offices, and multi-floor buildings.

❄️ Air Conditioning

Air Conditioner Services

Supply & installation of split, cassette, and ducted AC systems. Regular servicing, gas refilling & repair for all major brands.

🔥 Fire Safety

Fire Extinguishers

Supply of certified fire extinguishers for commercial & residential use, plus refilling, inspection, and compliance servicing.

🔧 Maintenance

Annual Maintenance Contracts

Scheduled AMC packages for CCTV systems, networking equipment, and air conditioners — ensuring uninterrupted operation year-round.

🛡️ Consultation

IT & Security Consultation

On-site assessment and planning for camera placement, network design, and fire safety requirements tailored to your premises.

🔋 UPS & Power Backup

UPS Systems

Supply and installation of UPS units for homes, offices, and businesses. Ensures uninterrupted power for computers, CCTV systems, and critical equipment during outages.

🏠 Home Appliances

Home Appliances

Supply of a wide range of home appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, televisions, and more — from trusted brands at competitive prices.

📞 PABX & IP Telephone

PABX & IP Telephone

Supply and installation of PABX switchboards and IP telephone systems for offices, hotels, and enterprises — enabling seamless internal and external communication.

🚪 Access Control

Door Access Control Systems

Biometric, card-based, and PIN access control solutions for offices, warehouses, and restricted areas — securing your premises with full audit trails.
